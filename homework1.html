Vegan Cookies Recipe

Recipe made by me

Prep Time

1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 cup + 2 tbs non-dairy milk
  • 2 tbs golden flax meal
  • 1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1.5c bread flour
  • 3/4 c toasted coconut flour
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp molasses
  • 1 c + 2 tbs semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 c shredded coconut, unsweetened

Cooking Instructions

  1. Toast the shredded coconut at 300F for 2 mins
  2. Curdle the non-dairy milk mixture
  3. Whisk together the dry ingredients
  4. Whisk together the flavor building ingredients
  5. Mix the dough
  6. Form the dough into balls, place them on the cookie sheet and top with coconut. Bake to perfection
